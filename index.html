My Game Dev Journey

From Gamer to Creator

After 10th grade, my love for video games evolved into a passion for creating them. Fascinated by the intricate worlds and challenges games offered, I wanted to understand how they were made. I began with Python and Java, creating a simple Snake Game.

This small success hooked me, realizing I could build games using the same logic I enjoyed as a player. As my skills grew, I tackled more complex projects like a Brick Breaker Game in Java, learning about game physics and user experience. Discovering Blender and Unreal Engine opened up 3D game development.

This transition was challenging but exciting, involving not just code, but bringing visual elements to life. My most significant project, Cyber Run, a 3D adventure platformer, allowed me to apply everything I'd learned. Publishing it on itch.io was a proud moment, realizing a dream years in the making.

Expanding my horizons, I ventured into NFTs, creating and publishing a unique collection on OpenSea. This project bridged game development with blockchain technology. Throughout this journey, my passion for creating enjoyable experiences has remained constant. From being a gamer to becoming a developer, it's been incredibly rewarding.

This is just the beginning - there are more games to build, worlds to create, and experiences to share.
